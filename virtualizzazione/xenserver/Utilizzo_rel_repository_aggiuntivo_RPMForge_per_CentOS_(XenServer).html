Scaricare il pacchetto rpmforge-release in base alla propria piattaforma (uname -i):

  • http://packages.sw.be/rpmforge-release/rpmforge-release-0.5.2-2.el5.rf.i386.rpm
  • http://packages.sw.be/rpmforge-release/rpmforge-release-0.5.2-2.el5.rf.x86_64.rpm

Importare la chiave GPG del repo con il comando

rpm --import http://apt.sw.be/RPM-GPG-KEY.dag.txt

Installare il pacchetto scaricato con 

rpm -i rpmforge-release-0.5.2-2.el5.rf.*.rpm
